About Me

Hello! I'm Owen Matejka, a Computer Science and Engineering student with a passion for technology and the outdoors.

As the president of the SCU Ski & Snowboard Club, I enjoy fostering a community of ski and snowboard enthusiasts by organizing events and trips to the mountains. I am also a proud member of the Men's Ultimate Frisbee team and the Association for Computing Machinery.

When I'm not busy with my academics or involved in club activities, you can find me hiking in the hills surrounding the Bay, building random things, and, of course, carving down the slopes.
